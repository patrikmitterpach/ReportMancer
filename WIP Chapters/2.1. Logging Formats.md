As different development teams have different requirements, so too do their needs for logging differ. Some applications may require a large amount of logs to be processed in a short amount of time, some place large importance on the data integrity and log all actions taken by the software multiple times on multiple different places. Some teams may be locked into working with specific formats based on their software vendors, customers or due to regulations defined by the law.

For the reasons detailed above, several logging formats have emerged from the developer community. The specifics and abilities of each format can differ greatly. The following chapters detail the commonly used formats, as well as the formats for the specific reporting standards that the application developed in the practical part of this thesis standardizes.
